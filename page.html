Celebration

Friday, 4th September, The Big Day! Held at Villa Gessa in the garden. We will be spending the days leading up to the event visiting farmers, wineries and picking up produce in the region to prepare and cook a meal for you all.


We welcome people from 2:00 pm, with the "ceremony" at 2:30pm sharp. We will be hosting the lunch and sitting down from 3:00pm and will continue on into the evening at Villa Gessa before heading into Bologna to go wineing and dancing, for those wishing. We will organise transport to take us from the Villa to downtown Bologna at night.


We will be well prepared for lunch, but those willing to help with a few simple tasks, please let us know.

Recovery

We are still finalising a recovery for Saturday, 5th September, which will most likely be in Bologna, San Giovanni in Persiceto or Savigno for those wishing to join. We will send out another message closer to the date.

Dress Code

Outdoor lunch, late Italian summertime (think nearly 30 degrees). We would love to see you in something fabulous and fruity, or something you've always wanted to wear in the European summer.


Lachlan will be wearing a suit with a tie; Georgia will be in an informal wedding dress. Dress for the weather and whatever you're comfortable in to have a good time!

Parking & Transportation

Villa Gessa is a 35-min drive from Bologna, and the closest train station is Muffa (5min drive / 20-minute walk).


We think most of you will be staying in Bologna or in the surrounding hills/towns, and parking will be available if you're hiring a car! For those staying in the surrounding small towns, there's a variety of local transport and taxis available.

Dietary Requirements

Please let us know of any dietary needs or allergies when you RSVP, and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Accommodation

There are numerous motels, hotels, and Airbnbs in Bologna and the surrounding Bologna Hills. (Closest towns are Mugga, Bazzano, Corallo-Sveglia, Crespellano for some countryside accommodation, or stay in the city centre of Bologna where the evening will finish!). Lachy and Georgia will be staying at Villa Gessa and spending time in the surrounding areas from the 2nd to the 7th September, we welcome anyone to stay nearby for the same period as us.

Gifts

We think that your presence in Bologna will be the biggest gift. But if you wish to give us something, we will have a wishing well on the day.

We're so excited to see you there!!
